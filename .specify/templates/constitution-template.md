## Governance

### Amendment Procedure

1. Any contributor MAY propose an amendment by opening a pull request
   against this file.
2. The amendment MUST be reviewed by at least one project maintainer.
3. Breaking changes (principle removal or redefinition) increment the
   MAJOR version; new principles or materially expanded guidance
   increment MINOR; clarifications and wording fixes increment PATCH.

### Versioning Policy

This constitution follows semantic versioning. Every change to this
document MUST be accompanied by a version bump and an entry in the
Sync Impact Report at the top of the file.

### Compliance Review

All feature specifications and plans MUST reference the principles
they uphold. Any specification that contradicts a principle MUST be
rejected or the constitution MUST be formally amended first.
